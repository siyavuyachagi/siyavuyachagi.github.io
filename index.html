Biography

My name is Siyavuya “CeeJay” Chagi, born and raised in Bizana, South Africa. I first fell in love with technology while at Emdeni JSS, creating game mods and patches with friends for fun, but also to see how far we could push the limits of what already existed. That curiosity eventually grew into a full-blown passion for building things from the ground up.

I’m a full-stack developer with 4+ years of building scalable web apps that don’t just look good but actually perform. My core toolkit is C# .NET on the backend and Vue.js / Nuxt.js on the frontend, though I’m always experimenting with new tech that pushes projects forward.

I love turning complex problems into clean, efficient solutions. Over the years, I’ve shipped products with diverse teams, optimized apps for speed and scalability, and mentored juniors to help them grow faster. Cloud architecture and performance tuning are where I nerd out the most.

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me exploring new tech, contributing to open-source, or sharing knowledge through blogs and community meetups.

My golden rule? Write impactful, maintainable code that future developers won’t curse me out for. 😅
